Pikachu

Type: Electric
Weakness: Ground
  • It is an electric-type pokemon introduced in Generation 1.
  • It can generate powerful electricity and have cheek sacs that are extra soft and super stretchy.
  • It evolves from Pichu and evolves into Raichu when exposed to a Thunder Stone.
  • It also played the role of a detective in the movie "Pokemon Detective Pikachu".
