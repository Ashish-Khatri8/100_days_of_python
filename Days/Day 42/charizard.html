Charizard

Type Fire Flying
Weakness Water Electric Rock
  • It is a dual-type Fire/Flying Pokemon introduced in Generation 1.
  • It spits fire that is hot enough to melt boulders.
  • It evolves from Charmeleon and is the final form of Charmander.
  • It even appeared in the movie "Pokemon Detective Pikachu".

Evolution:

-----> ----->
Charmander -----> Charmeleon -----> Charizard

Forms:

Charizard has 3 other forms.

  • It can Mega evolve into two forms:
    • Mega Charizard X using Charizardite X.
    • Mega Charizard Y using Charizardite Y.
  • It has a Gigantamax form.
